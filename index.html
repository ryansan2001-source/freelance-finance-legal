Freelance Finance Privacy Policy

Effective July 5, 2026 · Last updated July 5, 2026

Freelance Finance is developed by Ryan McGee. The short version: the app does not collect any of your data, and we never receive it.

Summary

  • We collect nothing. No analytics, no advertising, no tracking, no accounts.
  • Everything you enter (income, expenses, client names, receipt photos, and tax settings) is stored on your device.
  • If you turn on iCloud sync, that data is stored in your own private iCloud account and synced across your devices by Apple. We have no access to it.
  • The app works fully offline. The only network activity is Apple's iCloud sync, and only if you enable it.

Information We Collect

None. Freelance Finance does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information with us or with any third party. There is no account to create and no sign-in. We have no servers that receive your data.

Information Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following locally on your device so it can do its job:

  • Financial records you enter: income and expense amounts, client names, notes, dates, and expense categories.
  • Receipt photos you choose to attach to an expense.
  • Preferences: your tax rate, currency, and app settings.

This information never leaves your device except through the optional iCloud sync described below. You can delete any of it at any time by removing the relevant entries, or delete all of it by deleting the app.

Optional iCloud Sync

If you enable Sync to iCloud in the app's Settings, your financial records and receipt photos are stored in your private iCloud database and kept in sync across your Apple devices. This uses Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) service:

  • The data is associated with your Apple ID, not with us.
  • We cannot see, access, or retrieve this data. It is handled between your devices and Apple under Apple's infrastructure.
  • Apple's handling of this data is governed by the Apple Privacy Policy.
  • iCloud sync is off by default. You can turn it off at any time in Settings.

Device Permissions

The app asks for these system permissions only to provide specific features. In each case the data is used on-device and is not sent to us:

  • Photos: so you can attach a receipt photo to an expense. The app uses the system photo picker and only receives the photo you select.
  • Calendar (add events only): so you can add quarterly tax deadlines to your calendar. The app can only add events; it cannot read your calendar.
  • Notifications: so the app can remind you of upcoming tax deadlines. These reminders are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device.

Receipt text recognition (reading the total and date from a receipt photo) runs entirely on your device using Apple's on-device Vision framework. Receipt images are never uploaded for processing.

Third Parties

Freelance Finance contains no third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or software development kits (SDKs). We do not share data with anyone, because we never receive any.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children and does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction).

Data Retention and Deletion

Because your data lives on your device (and, if enabled, in your own iCloud):

  • To delete individual records, remove them within the app.
  • To delete everything, delete the app from your device. To also remove synced copies, disable iCloud sync and delete the app's data from your iCloud storage in the device's Settings.

We hold no copies of your data and therefore have nothing to delete on our end.

Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and others)

Privacy laws such as the EU/UK GDPR and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) give you rights to access, correct, delete, and port your personal data, and to not be discriminated against for exercising those rights.

Because we do not collect or hold any of your personal information, there is nothing for us to access, disclose, correct, delete, or port on your behalf, since your data is under your own control on your device and in your own iCloud account. If you have any questions about these rights, contact us below.

We do not "sell" or "share" personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA, and we do not engage in cross-context behavioral advertising.

Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security (including device passcode and encryption) and, when iCloud sync is enabled, by Apple's iCloud security. Keep your device and Apple ID secured to protect your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and post the new version at this page. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
